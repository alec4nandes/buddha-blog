The Buddha's Advice for Calming the Mind

How to overcome our stressful default mode of thinking and find peace.

Mon, Apr 17, 2023 at 16:20

Featured sutta: With Meghiya (Udāna 4.1)

Humans haven't changed at all in the past 2,500 years. I may be writing this while sitting in a downtown café, sipping machine-brewed imported coffee and typing on my touchscreen laptop, but all this modern technology hasn't really changed me or anyone else. Just as it was in the time of the Buddha, most people go about their lives burdened by distracting self-centered thoughts.
